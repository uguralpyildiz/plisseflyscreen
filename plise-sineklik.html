Plisse Fly Screen Systems

27mm and 18mm Normal

This series is especially preferred for doors, with the mesh folding inside the frame on one side or both sides.

The pleated cassette is directly and easily fixed to the door or window frame using screws in the mounting holes located on the corner brackets.

27mm and 18mm Threshold-less

Typically used in door fly screens. It prevents tripping in high-traffic areas, does not deform due to its hardness, and provides long-lasting use. Ideal for use in gardens and terrace exits.

Available in three colors: White, Anthracite Gray, and Anodized.

18mm Internal Mount Series



The internal mount series does not require any drilling during installation, making it a practical solution for situations with limited space. It is applied between the window frame and window sash using metal or plastic clips. It can be easily inserted and removed. It cannot be applied if the window sash has a drip edge.

27mm Wing Profile

Wing Profile: This profile, which is mounted on the ends of the frame profile, allows the pleated mesh to move thanks to its special design. It traps the mesh inside, ensuring a longer lifespan.

27mm Frame Profile

The aluminum extrusion is done hot. During production, 6063 series billets are used. The billets are heated to 420-520°C, molds are heated to 450°C, and the profile coming out of the press is above 500°C. The profile is cooled, cold-stretched, and cut to the desired length. Then, it is heat-treated to strengthen the profiles. During this process, the profiles are kept at 185°C for 5 hours. Strengthened profiles are then sent to the powder coating line, anodizing line, wood transfer line, or mechanical processing line as per customer request. After the necessary processes are applied, the profiles are ready for shipment.

The frame profile: With a depth of 27 mm and a front face width of 39 mm, it provides a solid appearance. It can be considered the main profile of the pleated fly screen. It forms the skeleton of the pleated fly screen by joining corner brackets.

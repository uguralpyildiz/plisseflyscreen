Get to Know Us

About Us

At AkPlise, located in Sancaktepe, Istanbul, we maintain our leading position in the industry in the production and sale of pleated insect screens and pleated curtain materials. Our company provides comprehensive service by offering all necessary machines and apparatuses in the production process, in addition to high-quality materials.

Our comprehensive product range is carefully selected and developed to offer the most suitable solutions to meet the needs of our customers. Whether for homes or commercial spaces, our pleated insect screens and curtains, which combine aesthetics and functionality, make your living spaces more comfortable.

Quality

Our products and equipment are manufactured in accordance with high-quality standards.

Technology

We provide service with state-of-the-art production machines.

Full Support

We provide all the machines and apparatuses you need during the production process.

Our Mission

At AkPlise, our mission is to enhance the comfort and aesthetics of living spaces by providing high-quality pleated insect screens and pleated curtain materials to our customers. With our advanced production facility and expert team, we work to deliver the fastest and most effective solutions to our customers' demands, apply the highest quality standards in the industry, and maintain customer satisfaction at the highest level.

Our Vision

AkPlise's vision is to be a nationally and internationally recognized and preferred brand in the pleated insect screen and pleated curtain industry. With our continuous innovative approaches, customer-oriented service philosophy, and high-quality standards, we aim to strengthen our pioneering position in the industry. Through environmentally friendly production methods and technological investments, we aim to add value to living spaces by offering sustainable and long-lasting solutions.
We take pride in exporting to our global markets and delivering our high-quality products to customers worldwide. If you are looking for a reliable supplier across the globe, get in touch with us.

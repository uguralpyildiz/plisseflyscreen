Frequently Asked Questions

What is a pleated mosquito net and how does it work?

A pleated mosquito net is a type of net that can be easily closed when not in use and, when opened, prevents mosquitoes and other insects from entering. It is extremely easy to use and is mounted on window or door frames.

What is a pleated curtain?

Pleated curtains are types of curtains that open and close by folding, offering a modern and stylish look. They provide effective solutions for light control and privacy.

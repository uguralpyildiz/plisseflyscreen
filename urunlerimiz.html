AkPlise Products

Eyelet Punching and Setting Machine

The Eyelet Punching Machine is a specialized machine used in the production of screens and curtains. Special banded strips are used to keep the net and curtain in place during production. This machine performs the punching process for nets, curtains, and strips. With its special die, symmetrical holes are made in the strip net and curtain in eyelet size.

The Eyelet Setting Machine is the same machine as the punching machine, but with different dies. This machine is used for setting the eyelets. The punching process performed with the previous machine is completed with this machine, ensuring the installation of eyelets on the strips of net and curtain. The net and curtain are then prepared for gathering (threading).

Curtain Cutting Machine

The Curtain Cutting Machine is used in the production of screens and curtains. Special banded strips are used to keep the net and curtain in place during production. This machine performs the cutting process for nets, curtains, and strips. With its special die, symmetrical holes are made in the strip net and curtain in eyelet size.

Thread Hole Punching Machine

The Thread Hole Punching Machine is an important machine for screen and curtain production. Also known as a column drill, it ensures that nets and curtains are punched symmetrically with its special tips and dies. The machine is fixed on the production table. The punching process is performed with a special die prepared parallel to the meter and front of the machine.

Curtain Thread Adjustment Stand

The Thread Adjustment Stand: After curtain production, the curtain is customized according to specific measurements. The key feature of this stand is that it prepares the curtain system for assembly with its special band system after production. The curtain band system is placed and clips are locked with the measurement rail. During assembly, clips are placed with either screwing or gluing, completing the assembly process.

Rope

The Pleated Screen PVC Band Strip adheres to the screen's net, providing a strong connection between the net and aluminum. The main feature of this product is that it is made from heat-resistant PVC material. The double-sided tape on the strip maintains its properties under various weather conditions, both hot and cold. After production, our company tests the durability of the strips under hot conditions and applies special tapes for this product. AK PLİSE branded PVC Strips come with a 10-year warranty.

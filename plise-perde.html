Pleated Blinds Systems

In recent years, changing lifestyle standards have allowed people to spend more time at home. In this context, people are making efforts to beautify their living spaces and lead a more comfortable life. One of the most important elements is Pleated Blinds (Glass Balcony Blinds). Although it is called Glass Balcony Blinds, the usage area is much broader. It has two main features. The first is its ability to block unwanted sunlight, and the second is its ability to obscure unwanted views. It adds a unique atmosphere to spaces with various colors and fabric options. AK Pleated Blinds Company is one of the leading organizations in the industry with its rich color and fabric options. All systems and components are its patented products.

Lina Series

It is the most important series in our portfolio of 40 colors. There are 16 color alternatives available, with a coverage and opacity rate of 55-60%. It is the most preferred series due to its competitive price. Its special pattern provides an aesthetic look to your spaces.

Hira Series

It is the series referred to as Black Out in the industry. It provides 100% coverage. It is specially produced with a Teflon material placed between two different fabrics. Currently, it is available in 4 colors. Due to its double color fabric, it can be used with double-sided colors in different shades. One of its most important features is that it is used as a boxed system on the ceiling in winter gardens, especially abroad, to help block sunlight. It is one of our most preferred series.

Sun Series

We can consider it as the closest series to our Hira series (Black Out) in terms of opacity. The blackout rate is around 90%. It is available in four colors. The fabric is thicker compared to other series. It is an economical alternative to Black Out fabrics for applications.
